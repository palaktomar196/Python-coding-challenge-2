Question:- Scenrio:- A calendar application checks leap year.
Task:- write a program to determine whether a year is a leap year.
Example:- Input:- 2024


                        Output:-Leap year 
   Answer:- year = int(input("Enter year: "))


if (year % 400 == 0) or (year % 4 == 0 and year % 100 != 0):
    print("Leap year")
else:
    print("Not a leap year")

Question:- Scenrio:- a store tracks daily sales 
Task:- write a program to calculate total weekly sales 
Example:- input:- (100,200,150,300,250,400,100)
Output:- 1500


Answer :-  sales = list(map(int, input("Enter weekly sales (comma separated): ").split(",")))


total = 0


for i in sales:
    total = total + i


print(total)
